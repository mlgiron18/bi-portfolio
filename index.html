Sample Project

NYC Multifamily Energy Performance Dashboard

Built using real data from NYC Open Data: Local Law 84 Energy & Water Disclosure (datasets 5zyy-y8am & 7x5e-2fxh), covering 354 multifamily buildings across all 5 boroughs for calendar years 2021–2022.

Dashboard tracking energy performance of NYC multifamily housing using the city's mandatory benchmarking data under Local Law 84. Shows borough-level Energy Star scores, site energy use intensity (EUI), year-over-year performance trends, and GHG emissions — the kind of reporting that organizations like KC3 use to identify buildings most in need of efficiency upgrades and decarbonization investment.
